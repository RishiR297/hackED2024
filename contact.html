SERVICES TO CONTACT.

Within this section, we outline essential services to contact for various needs and inquiries. Whether you require assistance, information, or support, the following services are readily available to address your concerns. Explore the options below to connect with the appropriate professionals and resources.


Need immediate help?

  • Call 911, or
  • Visit the nearest urgent care center or emergency room.

Need someone to talk to?

Access 24/7

  • For assistance with Adult Addiction & Mental Health concerns, please call 780-424-2424. The helpline is accessible 24/7.

Health Link

  • Contact 811 to engage in a conversation with a Registered Nurse regarding your health inquiries.

Looking for a service?

Health Link

  • Dial 811 to connect with an Information & Referral Specialist who can assist you in finding the service that suits your needs. Our support is accessible 24/7 to provide assistance.
